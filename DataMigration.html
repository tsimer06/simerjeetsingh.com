I work closely with stakeholders to gather and document detailed migration requirements, followed by extensive data mapping to align legacy fields with Oracle Cloud Fusion, establishing clear connections and ensuring data consistency. Additionally, I define validation and cleansing protocols to address data quality issues proactively. Throughout the project, I facilitate User Acceptance Testing (UAT) sessions, validating that the migrated data meets business requirements and tracking any issues for resolution. I provide regular project updates, keeping stakeholders informed and aligning project milestones with their expectations. To ensure compliance, I address security and governance standards while working on data migration. Post-migration, I conduct a final validation to confirm data integrity, deliver training sessions to end users, and create detailed documentation. This approach supports a smooth transition to Oracle Cloud Fusion and empowers users to manage data effectively in the new system.

Azure's AI and ML services, including Azure Machine Learning, empower businesses to leverage predictive analytics and custom models. Additionally, its extensive security and compliance features, such as Azure Policy and Security Center, are particularly beneficial for industries with stringent regulatory requirements.
