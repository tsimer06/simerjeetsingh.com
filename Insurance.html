Insurance Claim Project

🛡️
01
Requirements gathering & documentation
Led the initial stages by gathering and documenting detailed business requirements from stakeholders to ensure a clear understanding of project goals. This process focused on aligning the claims application with specific business objectives, prioritising accuracy and operational efficiency throughout.
02
Gap analysis
Conducted a thorough gap analysis to identify inefficiencies within the existing claims process. The findings informed targeted improvements to streamline workflows, reduce manual intervention, and enhance alignment between the operational process and business objectives.
03
Collaboration with development & testing teams
Worked closely with developers and testers to integrate policy, claims, and customer data seamlessly. Ensured each component was well-coordinated to deliver a holistic, user-friendly solution that meaningfully improved the claims process end to end.
04
Stakeholder workshops
Facilitated regular workshops with key stakeholders to maintain alignment, gather feedback, and make timely adjustments. This collaborative approach surfaced concerns early and sustained stakeholder engagement throughout the full project lifecycle.
05
Product backlog management
Managed and refined the product backlog to prioritise high-impact features and updates, keeping the delivery team focused on the most valuable work. Clear backlog organisation supported timely feature delivery and reduced ambiguity across sprints.
06
User acceptance testing (UAT)
Coordinated User Acceptance Testing to validate that the application met user expectations and functional requirements. Gathered and addressed feedback to make final adjustments before deployment, ensuring high user satisfaction and a smooth go-live.
