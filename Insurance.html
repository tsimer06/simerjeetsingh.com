Insurance Claim Project
I led the end-to-end process of developing and implementing an insurance claims application. My role involved gathering and documenting business requirements from stakeholders, conducting gap analysis to streamline the claims process, and aligning the solution with business objectives. ...I collaborated closely with developers and testers, ensuring the integration of policy, claims, and customer data. Throughout the project, I facilitated workshops with key stakeholders, managed the product backlog, and coordinated User Acceptance Testing (UAT) to ensure the system met user needs. The project successfully enhanced operational efficiency, reduced processing times, and improved overall customer experience through automation and streamlined workflows.
