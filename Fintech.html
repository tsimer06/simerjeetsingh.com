Project 01 · CRM

CRM Platform Integration & Optimisation

Collaborated with retail operations, marketing, and IT stakeholders to gather and document detailed CRM integration requirements. Conducted a thorough analysis of the existing customer data landscape, identifying fragmented data sources, duplicate records, and manual handoff points that were limiting campaign effectiveness and customer visibility.
Developed detailed data mapping documentation aligning legacy customer records with the new CRM data model. Defined field-level transformation rules, cleansing protocols, and deduplication logic to ensure a high-quality, unified customer profile was established from day one of go-live.
Led API analysis and integration design to connect the CRM with POS systems, eCommerce platforms, and the loyalty programme engine. Documented API specifications and worked closely with developers to ensure reliable, real-time data flow between systems, eliminating silos and enabling a single customer view across all retail channels.
Facilitated cross-functional workshops with sales, marketing, customer service, and IT teams to align on CRM usage standards, data governance policies, and reporting requirements. Developed training materials and supported change management activities to drive adoption across the retail business.
Coordinated UAT sessions with end users across store operations and the digital team to validate CRM workflows, data accuracy, and reporting dashboards. Tracked and resolved defects pre-launch and established post-implementation KPIs to monitor ongoing system performance and data quality.

Project 02 · Payments

Payment Gateway System Integration for Retail

Led the end-to-end integration of a multi-channel payment gateway solution across in-store POS terminals, the eCommerce platform, and the mobile app. Managed requirements, vendor relationships, and delivery timelines to ensure a cohesive payment experience across all retail touchpoints — in line with both business goals and PCI-DSS compliance standards.
Gathered and documented business and technical requirements from finance, IT, and operations teams. Produced detailed functional specifications and use cases covering payment flows, refund and chargeback processes, multi-currency handling, and fraud detection logic — providing a clear, traceable reference for development and testing.
Worked with the security and compliance team to define fraud detection rules and ensure the payment gateway met PCI-DSS Level 1 standards. Documented compliance requirements and coordinated with third-party auditors to validate controls, tokenisation protocols, and data encryption practices prior to go-live.
Facilitated integration workshops with payment service providers (Stripe, Adyen) and acquiring banks to align on API specifications, settlement workflows, and reconciliation formats. Maintained a risk register and issue log throughout, ensuring all integration dependencies were tracked and resolved ahead of the phased rollout.
Oversaw UAT and performance testing across all payment channels, simulating peak retail transaction volumes to validate system stability. Coordinated the phased rollout — starting with eCommerce, then in-store — with rollback procedures in place. Post-launch monitoring confirmed improved transaction success rates and a significant drop in failed payment incidents.
